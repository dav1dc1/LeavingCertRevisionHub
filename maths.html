Advanced Maths Topics

Algebra

Equations & Functions: Detailed study of linear, quadratic, and polynomial equations; function notation, domain, range, and graphing techniques.

Geometry

Euclidean Geometry: Properties of shapes, theorems and proofs, constructions, and circle theorems with worked examples.

Calculus

Limits & Continuity: Explanation of limits, continuity definitions, and epsilon-delta proofs.

Differentiation & Integration: Techniques for differentiation, applications to rates of change, integration methods, and area calculations with sample problems.

Statistics & Probability

Data Analysis: Mean, median, mode, variance, standard deviation, and data interpretation.

Probability: Fundamental probability rules, combinations, permutations, and sample exam questions.

Trigonometry

Trigonometric Functions: In-depth study of sine, cosine, tangent and their inverses.

Identities & Equations: Use of Pythagorean identities, angle sum and double-angle formulas, with practice problems.
